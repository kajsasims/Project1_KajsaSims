SKIRT STITCH JEANS

A bold statement in avant-garde fashion, the Skirt Stitch Jeans blend structured denim with flowing fabric for an unexpected and artistic contrast. The light-wash denim base provides a classic and sturdy foundation, while the attached pink textile disrupts convention with its raw, unfinished edges and intricate embroidered text. The embroidery forms a circular pattern, adding depth and conceptual intrigue, while the cascading pink fabric introduces movement and texture. The juxtaposition of structured denim and soft, draped textile creates a striking visual, making these pants a wearable piece of contemporary art.

Features:

-Light-wash denim base with structured seams

-Integrated pink textile with raw, deconstructed edges

-Embroidered circular text motif for artistic detail

-Asymmetrical design with flowing fabric accent

Perfect for those who embrace experimental fashion, the Skirt Stitch Jeans challenge traditional silhouettes, offering a unique and thought-provoking style.
